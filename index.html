About

Who We Are?

At Notch India Projects, we pride ourselves on being a trusted partner in India's road infrastructure development.

What We Do?

We specialize in a wide range of infrastructure services, including overlaying, road widening, forming new bypasses, and constructing major bridges.

Why We Do It?

At Notch India Projects, we’re committed to enhancing India’s infrastructure for better connectivity and safety.

Since When?

Finance performance

Notch India Projects has demonstrated extraordinary growth over the past three years, with consistent year-on-year increases in revenue. Our turnover has grown by 355% from financial year 2021-2022 to 2023-2024, reflecting our strong position in the industry and our commitment to delivering quality infrastructure solutions. In the upcoming financial year, we project a further 32% growth, driven by our expansion into new markets, strategic partnerships, and an increasing portfolio of high-value projects. This forecast underscores our long-term vision of sustainable growth and continuous improvement in all aspects of our business operations.
